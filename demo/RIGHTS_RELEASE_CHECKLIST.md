# Entrant, rights, licensing and publication sign-off

This is a human release gate derived from the official
[eligibility, ownership and submission rules](https://qwencloud-hackathon.devpost.com/rules).
It is an operational checklist, not legal advice. Automation cannot truthfully
complete these attestations for the entrant.

## Entrant and eligibility

- [ ] Every entrant is at least the age of majority where they reside and is
  eligible under the jurisdiction, sanctions and restricted-party rules.
- [ ] No entrant has a disqualifying sponsor, administrator, judge, promotion-entity
  or apparent conflict of interest.
- [ ] A team/organization submission has one authorized representative and every
  listed member approved the entry.
- [ ] Country, legal identity and Qwen Cloud account information are entered
  directly into Devpost, not committed to the repository.
- [ ] The project did not receive disqualifying financial or preferential support
  from the sponsor or administrator.

## Ownership and reuse boundary

- [ ] The entrant owns the submission-specific code, story, diagrams, screenshots,
  recording, narration and visual design, except for clearly licensed components.
- [ ] The existing Archon name/context and ported extraction/analysis patterns are
  disclosed in [`SUBMISSION.md`](./SUBMISSION.md); reused context is not claimed as
  new competition work.
- [ ] MemoryAgent and Autopilot are unique and substantially different entries, with
  separate codebases, track functionality, demos, screenshots and stories.
- [ ] No real customer, employee, payroll, invoice or other private data appears in
  submitted fixtures or media without documented authority and consent.

## Software and service licenses

- [ ] Root [`LICENSE`](../LICENSE) is MIT and GitHub detects it on the public landing page.
- [ ] Direct and transitive open-source dependencies were reviewed for compatible
  licenses and all required attribution/notice/source obligations are satisfied.
- [ ] Qwen Cloud, Alibaba Cloud, OpenAI-compatible SDK and every third-party API or
  service are used under current account terms that permit this public submission,
  judging and the shown functionality.
- [ ] No vendor logo, badge or trademark artwork is used beyond permission; product
  names are presented accurately and any organizer-provided marks follow the
  organizer's brand rules.
- [ ] No proprietary code, dataset, document template, font or stock asset was copied
  into the repository or media without a documented license.

## Video, image, voice and publicity rights

- [ ] Every image, diagram, icon and thumbnail is original or has a recorded license.
- [ ] Music is omitted or owned/licensed for public competition and promotional use;
  a streaming-platform library track is not assumed portable.
- [ ] Every human voice/likeness has a release covering the organizer's stated
  judging and promotional use. If synthetic speech is used, both the selected
  service/voice and any fallback path permit publication and promotional reuse.
- [ ] Captions and translations were created or authorized for publication.
- [ ] No third-party trademark, copyrighted media, personal identifier or private
  browser/cloud detail appears in any frame, thumbnail, caption or metadata.
- [ ] Evidence of permissions, invoices, terms snapshots or releases is retained in
  an entrant-controlled private location; secrets or personal documents are never committed.

## Accuracy, safety and final publication

- [ ] Every claim in the form, story, screenshots, video, captions and posts maps to
  [`docs/CLAIM_EVIDENCE_MATRIX.md`](../docs/CLAIM_EVIDENCE_MATRIX.md).
- [ ] Synthetic/developer-labelled evidence is labelled as such; no fixture score is
  described as production accuracy, independent expert validation or prevalence.
- [ ] The video shows the project functioning, is English or English-translated,
  strictly below three minutes, and publicly accessible on YouTube, Vimeo or Youku.
- [ ] The public repository contains the source, assets, instructions, detectable
  license, architecture and Alibaba/Qwen code proof required to evaluate the project.
- [ ] The app, repository, video and any judge credential will remain free and
  unrestricted through August 11, 2026 at 2:00 PM PDT.
- [ ] The final repository and media contain no malicious code, secret, hidden
  executable, unexpected archive or unsafe external download.
- [ ] The complete Devpost draft was previewed logged out, on desktop and mobile
  widths, and every link was opened from the preview.
